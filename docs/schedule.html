Note

Textbook reading assignments in the Watson & Breedlove text, The Mind’s Machine will be indicated by the following shorthand: ‘MM pp 3-15’. This means to read pages 3 to 15.

Note that the text covers material we won’t cover in class. If we don’t cover material in class, you aren’t responsible for it. That said, you may find it interesting to learn and know about anyway.
